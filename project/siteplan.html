Côte d'Ivoire HealthCare | Site Plan

Site Name :

Côte d'Ivoire HealthCare

This name is reflect the content of the website because I will provide information about the country's healthcare system. Domain: ivory-healthcare.com

Site Purpose

The website will be a platform dedicated to relaying WHO information about the country’s healthcare system. It will highlight the most common and relevant health information in Côte d'Ivoire, drawn from the vast amount of available data. This includes essential health service coverage, maternal care (such as indicators related to maternal mortality and prenatal care), and useful health tips.

Scenarios

  • What are the most common and relevant health information in Côte d'Ivoire ?
  • What are the required and recommended vaccinations for Côte d'Ivoire ?
  • Where and How can contact WHO in Côte d'Ivoire ?
  • How can someone contact you ?
